Burns

Cool the area with running water for 10 minutes. Do not use ice.

Fractures

Immobilize the area with a splint and seek medical help.

Choking

Give 5 back blows between the shoulder blades followed by abdominal thrusts.

CPR

30 chest compressions and 2 rescue breaths until help arrives.

Snake Bite

Keep the victim calm and the bitten area still. Do not suck the venom.
